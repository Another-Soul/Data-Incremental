v0.1 (20.10.2023)
    Added 3 first Bytes upgrades: Binary Encoder, Multi-processing and Data Cascade. Each next upgrade boosts the previous one.

    Added Efficiency, which boosts Bytes gain at the cost of your system. When you store more than 1e6 Bytes, you can reset all Bytes upgrades
    and Bytes in exchange for Efficiency. Efficiency is measured in percentage, each % corresponds to +×0.01 boost to Bytes. The initial
    Efficiency is 100% (×1).
